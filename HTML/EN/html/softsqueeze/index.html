SoftSqueeze emulates the Squeezebox and Transporter on Windows, Mac OSX and Unix computers.

In Squeezebox 7.4 SoftSqueeze has been replaced with SqueezePlay. SoftSqueeze is still being developed and maintained by the Squeezebox community. For more information on SoftSqueeze please visit the SoftSqueeze page on Sourceforge.
